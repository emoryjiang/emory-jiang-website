I was a mentor for the IgniteCS mentorship program. This program introduced female high school students to coding. Our program aimed to shatter the social stigma that surrounded woman in computer science. My personal job was to lead the workshops on Processing. This included producing helpful handouts as well as providing lectures for the students.
